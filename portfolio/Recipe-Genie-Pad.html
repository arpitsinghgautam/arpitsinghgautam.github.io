Recipe Genie Pad

By Arpit Singh Gautam Mar 15, 2020

Recipe Genie Pad is a revolutionary web app designed to make cooking and eating more accessible, enjoyable, and sustainable. It leverages advanced technology to identify dishes from photos and provide recipe suggestions, making it easy for food lovers to discover new cuisines and explore their culinary skills.

In addition, the app offers automated food delivery search and charitable food donation suggestions, allowing users to order their favorite dishes or donate excess food to those in need with just a few clicks.

Working

I built Recipe Genie Pad using Python and various Python libraries such as Streamlit, Keras, Tensorflow, Selenium, and Beautiful Soup. The image recognition model was trained using a pre-trained model, Inception V3, and fine-tuned on a food image dataset.

I integrated the app with Streamlit to create a user-friendly interface and used Selenium to automate the food delivery search feature. I used Domain.com for the domain name..

Future Aspects

The future of Recipe Genie Pad looks bright, with plans to expand its image recognition capabilities to include more types of food and dishes. The app will also include a feature that suggests wine pairings for the recipes suggested by the app.

Additionally, Recipe Genie Pad plans to explore ways to make the food delivery search feature more precise and customizable for users. Other potential future features include a meal planning feature and an ingredient substitution tool.

Summary

Recipe Genie Pad is a cutting-edge web app that uses advanced technology to simplify cooking and eating. It can identify dishes from photos and suggest relevant recipes, as well as provide automated food delivery search and charitable food donation suggestions.

Built using Python and various Python libraries, Recipe Genie Pad is user-friendly and accessible to everyone. Its future plans include expanding its image recognition capabilities, adding a wine pairing feature, and improving the food delivery search feature's precision and customization options.
